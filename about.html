Hi!
Welcome to KaptureCon—the ultimate K-pop convention! KaptureCon isn’t just an event—it’s a movement that brings the passion, energy, and creativity of this global phenomenon here to D.C.
Our
Vision
We believe K-pop is more than music—it’s a community. Our goal is to foster inclusivity, celebrate diversity, and showcase the talent and creativity of fans and beyond. With KaptureCon, we’re building a vibrant, welcoming space where everyone can celebrate their love for K-pop together.
Our
Mission
KaptureCon is dedicated to celebrating and connecting the global K-Pop community by fostering creative exchange, engagement, and artistic expression. We aim to provide a dynamic, immersive experience where fans and artists come together to share their passion for K-Pop. Through innovative programming, performances, and interactive events, KaptureCon strives to create lasting connections and unforgettable memories for all attendees, capturing the essence of K-Pop in the heart of the D.C Metropolitan Area.
Stay tuned for updates on our lineup, schedule, and ticketing info.

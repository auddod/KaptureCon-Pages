KPOP DANCE COMPETITION

Get ready to witness jaw-dropping moves and electrifying energy at the K-Pop Dance Competition happening from 3:30 PM to 5:00 PM! Watch talented dance crews and solo performers bring their favorite K-pop choreographies to life on stage in a thrilling showdown of skill, style, and stage presence.

AUDREY LANE WORKSHOP

Audrey Lane is a 21-year-old dancer based in Los Angeles, USA. She has been featured on Nickelodeon, Disney, Street Woman Fighter 2, and Street Girl Fighter 2. Audrey has also worked with renowned artists like Jennie, Jungkook, Jackson Wang, and Ciara. As an international instructor, she has taught all over the world, working with K-pop groups such as TXT, LE SSERAFIM, and woo!ah!.

ANALISSE WORKSHOP

Analisse is a highly accomplished professional choreographer and dancer with over 12 years of experience in the industry. Her choreography credits include ILLIT, Ozuna, Maluma, Pitbull, and more. She has also collaborated with renowned artists such as BTS, Jason Derulo, Chris Brown, Daddy Yankee, and Bad Bunny. Additionally, she has built a combined social media following of over 14 million across her personal and family brand.

MICHAEL WORKSHOP

Michael Le has over 10 years of professional choreography experience and a social media following exceeding 50 million. His work includes collaborations with artists like Enhypen, Le Sserafim, and BND, among others. He is now focused on becoming the largest dance creator globally.

RANDOM PLAY DANCE

Test your reflexes and dance skills in Random Play Dance (RPD), where songs play at random and only the bold stay dancing! Step up when you know the choreography and show your love for the music. A crowd favorite that's full of fun, spontaneity, and fan energy.

FAQ
Have questions about KaptureCon? Check out our FAQs for all the details you need!
Convention
What is the bag policy?
Bags that measure no larger than 12″ x 12″ x 6″ are permitted. Backpacks are not permitted. All parcels and bags being brought into The National Press Club are subject to inspection.
Can I bring in water?
Attendees are allowed to bring water bottles with caps into The National Press Club. Please ensure the bottles are sealed upon entry.
Can I bring in food?
No outside food is allowed in The National Press Club.
Can I bring lightsticks?
Yes, attendees can bring lightsticks.
What are the prohibited items?
  • Weapons of any kind, including mace, pepper spray, knives, sharp objects, bats, clubs, tasers, etc.
  • Bags larger than 12″ x 12″ x 6″ and backpacks of all sizes are prohibited
  • Professional camera equipment (e.g., detachable lens; lens greater than 3”; external flash; monopods; bipods; tripods; etc.)
  • Laptops, personal computers, and two-way radios
  • Laser pointers or flashlights
  • Illegal drugs/substances
  • Outside alcohol
  • Fireworks, missile-like objects, Frisbees, and any other form of projectiles
  • Outside food and beverages (excluding water bottles)
How can I get to The National Press Club by Metro?
  1. Take Metro to Metro Center.
  2. Take the 13th Street Exit, take escalator to 13th Street; you should be at the corner of 13th and G Streets.
  3. Walk one block south to F Street.
  4. Turn right (West) and walk one block to 14th Street.
  5. Turn left and walk downhill to the National Press Building lobby.
  6. Enter and take the elevators to the 13th Floor.
Where can I park?

For Guaranteed Parking Reservations
We recommend booking convenient and affordable parking in advance through SpotHero, the nation's leading parking reservations app.
To reserve your parking spot for KaptureCon, visit the National Press Club SpotHero Parking Page.


Valet Parking Options
Valet Parking is available at the JW Marriott hotel (next to the Press Club) or the Willard Hotel (across the street from the Press Club).

Concert
What is the bag policy?
Please be aware that for the safety of fellow guests, all purses and bags are subject to search.

Can I re-enter into DAR if I leave the venue?
No, re-entry to DAR Constitution Hall is not permitted.
How can I get to DAR by Metro?

From the Orange and Blue lines (A 10-15 minute walk):

  1. Take the Farragut West metro stop.
  2. Exit the station on 17th Street, and walk south on 17th Street towards Constitution. Turn right on D Street.
  3. The main entrance is in the middle of the block.

From the Red Line (A 10-15 minute walk):

  1. Take the Farragut North metro stop.
  2. Exit the station on 17th Street, and walk south on 17th Street towards Constitution. Turn right on D Street.
  3. The main entrance is in the middle of the block.
Where can I park?
For Guaranteed Parking Reservations
We recommend booking convenient and affordable parking in advance through SpotHero, the nation's leading parking reservations app.
To reserve your parking spot for KaptureCon, visit the DAR Convention Hall SpotHero Parking Page.

Metered street parking is available but limited. Monday through Saturday, two hour parking is enforced from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM for most meters.

There are parking garages located in the area on New York and Pennsylvania Avenues and 17th and 18th Streets.
Ticketing
Do I have to buy both a convention ticket and a concert ticket?
No! You have the option to buy a ticket just to the convention or buy a ticket just to the concert, or both!
Do I have to have a concert ticket to purchase the meet & greet?
No, you do not need to purchase a concert ticket to purchase the meet & greet.
Do I have to have a convention ticket to purchase the meet & greet?
Yes, you do need to purchase a convention ticket to purchase the meet & greet.
Can I get a refund on my concert and convention ticket?
No, unfortunately there will be no refunds to any tickets purchased.
